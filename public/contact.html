Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my package?

Use our online tracking system with your tracking ID (format: ESP-XXXXXXXXXXXX) or call our customer service line for assistance.

What are your shipping rates?

Shipping rates depend on destination, weight, dimensions, and service type. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance to protect your shipments. Insurance rates vary based on cargo value and destination.

What documents do I need?

Required documents vary by destination but typically include commercial invoice, packing list, and export/import permits.

How long does shipping take?

Transit times vary by service type and destination. Air freight: 1-5 days, Ocean freight: 15-45 days, Ground: 1-10 days.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes, our experienced customs team handles all clearance procedures to ensure smooth delivery of your shipments.
